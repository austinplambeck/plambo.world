/INTRO

Hey, everyone! I'm Austin Plambeck — a 24-year-old writer (soon-to-be author), web developer, and environmentalist from southern California.

I began my coding journey in 2021, learning through Codecademy and freeCodeCamp. Now I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm learning more every day.

plambo.world was built from the ground up with my own code, and it will function as a personal portfolio — containing both my coding projects and writing projects.

Recently, I created a new site (bleedingkyber.com) that will house my blog, short fiction, and other updates. Check out the category blocks below for a more detailed look!

Also(!) — I'm currently in the late stages of revising my fantasy novel, Tomorrow's Storm, and I’m happy to say I’ll be self-publishing it sometime in early 2022. You can read more about that by clicking here!

Thanks for stopping by! I hope you enjoy the site.

- Austin
