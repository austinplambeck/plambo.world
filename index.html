/INTRO

Hey, everyone! I'm Austin Plambeck — a 24-year-old writer (soon-to-be author), web developer, and environmentalist from southern California.

I began my coding journey in early 2021, learning through Codecademy. Now I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm learning more every day.

This website is the first project I’ve worked on independently and the goal is to have it function as a portfolio for both my coding work and my writing. You can check out the category blocks below for a more detailed look.

There will be a few different types of writing on here — from works of fiction to blog posts to my jumbled thoughts on a multitude of topics, including our chaotic world & environment.

Also(!) — I'm currently in the late stages of revising my fantasy novel, Tomorrow's Storm, and I’m happy to say I’ll be self-publishing it sometime in early 2022. You can read more about that by clicking here!

Thanks for stopping by! I hope you enjoy the site.

- Austin
